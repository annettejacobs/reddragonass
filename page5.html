SELF-DEFENSE

We provide specialized training in self-defensive countermeasures that involve defending oneself, one's property or the well being of another from harm.

Our self-defense system has been formulated over a period of 30 years and stems from a variety of martialarts styles.

The ability to protect yourself and loved ones is seen as a absolute must have skill from us here at RED DRAGON & ASSOCIATES. We have absolute faith in our system and that it is superior to any other system devised before or after.
