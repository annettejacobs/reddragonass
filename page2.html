RED DRAGON ASSOCIATES

I am Fanie Strydom, founder and Director of Red Dragon Associates company and started my business in 1995 in South Africa. As a Grand master in martial arts I decided to use my skills and experience to serve and protect people where needed with security, body guarding, debt collecting and investigating. 

I also wanted to share my love and passion for the Martial arts with young and old to enable them to protect themselves where necessary or grow the power, commitment and self control needed for this amazing sport art.

Victor Latsky, partner, joined me on this path in 2017 bringing his own experience and commitment to this field.
